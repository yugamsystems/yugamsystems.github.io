About

Hi, I'm Rohit Kumar Maskara.

I work with organizations to transform how they operate through strategic AI implementation and intelligent automation. For the past 15+ years, I've worked on one core problem: how do we make organizations more efficient, more agile, and more effective?

At Meta, I spent 8 years optimizing global customer experience operations, most recently as Senior Program Manager. At KPMG, I led transformation projects for Fortune 500 companies — redesigning processes that saved clients millions. At Vedanta Aluminium, I led production optimization that reduced scrap by 44% and energy consumption by 20%.

What I've learned across all of it: the best technology solutions fail without the right organizational setup. Success in the AI Yugam isn't about the coolest tools — it's about the right people, processes, and mindset to use them. That insight is why I started Yugam.

Principle 01

Principle 02

Principle 03

I'm not an advisor who disappears. I work with teams to build, test, and deploy.
