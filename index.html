Foundation Assessment

Before Implementation: Is Your Organization Ready?

Production-quality AI requires more than technology. You need the foundation.

Data Organization Is your data structured, documented, and accessible? Or fragmented across systems?

Process Clarity Do you have documented SOPs and knowledge bases? Or does process live in people's heads?

Team Readiness Are your teams prepared to work WITH AI? Or will they resist/misuse it?

Here's what I've learned: When the foundation is weak, you spend months rebuilding. If any of these are fragile, we start there.
