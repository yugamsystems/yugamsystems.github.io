# LinkedIn Post — "Subscription vs strategy"

76% of small businesses say they're "using AI."

14% have it embedded in their operations.

The difference between those two numbers is the difference between having a gym membership and being fit.

When I built the AI readiness diagnostics for Yugam, I included three questions that cut through the "we're using AI" claim:

→ Can two people in your company pull the same revenue number and get the same answer?
→ Could a new hire follow your most important process from a document — without asking anyone?
→ If your best operator called in sick for a week, which processes slow down or stop?

These aren't AI questions. They're foundation questions. And the answers tell you more about your AI readiness than any vendor demo.

The 14% who embedded AI in their operations answered these well before they picked a tool. The 62% who have a subscription but not a strategy? They skipped straight to the tool.

A subscription costs $20/month. A strategy costs effort. The 14% spent the effort.
