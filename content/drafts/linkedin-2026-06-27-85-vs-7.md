# LinkedIn Post — "The 85/7 gap"

BCG asked leaders whether adapting to AI at speed is critical for their business.

85% said yes.

Then they asked who's actually doing it.

7%.

I've spent a decade at Meta watching this exact gap play out — not with AI, but with every operational transformation. The pattern is the same: leadership knows what needs to happen, agrees it's urgent, then stalls because there's no structured path from "we should" to "we did."

The 7% who are moving didn't start with better AI tools. They started with three foundations:

1. One source of truth for the data that matters
2. Core processes documented well enough to automate
3. A team that was prepared, not ambushed

That's a 12-week commitment, not a 12-month transformation program.

The 85% who know but haven't acted? They're waiting for the perfect tool. The 7% didn't wait. They fixed the foundation and picked the tool second.
