Foundation Assessment

Three things I check before recommending any AI tool.

Data Organization
Is your data structured, documented, and accessible — or scattered across spreadsheets, inboxes, and tribal knowledge?
Process Clarity
Are your procedures documented — or does critical knowledge live in people's heads, vulnerable to turnover?
Team Readiness
Are your teams prepared to work alongside AI — or will adoption stall because the change was imposed, not enabled?
"I've watched teams spend six months building an AI solution on top of data that no one trusted. The AI worked. The data and processes around it didn't. They had to start over."
If data, process, or people are fragile — that's where I start. Every time.
