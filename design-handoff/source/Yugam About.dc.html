About

I've seen AI go wrong. That's why I do this.

I'm Rohit. For a decade I sat inside one of the world's largest technology companies, watching how transformation actually happens — and how often it doesn't. Before that, Fortune-500 governance work at KPMG. Before that, a manufacturing shop floor, where I learned to read how work really flows.

Yugam is the practice I built from that. I'm not a vendor and I don't resell tools. I tell founders and leadership teams the uncomfortable truth about whether their people, processes, and data are ready — before anyone spends a dollar on AI.
